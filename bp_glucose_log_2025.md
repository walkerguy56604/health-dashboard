# Blood Pressure Dashboard – Spikes & IHBs

## Key
- **IHB** = Irregular Heart Beat
- **High** = Spike above usual range (Systolic ≥ 130 mmHg or Diastolic ≥ 85 mmHg)
- Heart Rate in bpm
